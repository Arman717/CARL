Citing GenRL
=================

If you use GenRL your research, please cite us with the following Bibtex file:

.. code:: bash

        @misc{genRL,
  author    = {C. Benjamin and
               T. Eimer and
               F. Schubert and
               A. Biedenkapp and
               F. Hutter and
               B. Rosenhahn and
               M. Lindauer},
  title     = {Benchmarking GenRL Reinforcement Learning},
  howpublished = {https://github.com/automl/meta-gym},
  year      = {2021},
  month     = aug,
}
